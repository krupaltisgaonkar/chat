What we store

We store your profile picture, name, and email address.

How we store

We store your data on firebase. We save your chat messages, and only the last hundred messages of a chat will be shown.

Why we store

We store your data because this is a chatting website. We store what we said before.

What we do with your data.

Your data is just stored on our servers. We don't sell any of your information to anybody else.

How you should use this website.

This website is only the use of chatting, and we cannot be sued for the bullying of someone, as it is not our fault. However, if one of our admins misuses this website, they will be banned immediately.
